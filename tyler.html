About The Captain

Having grown up with this world class fishery in my backyard, and fishing every chance possible without a doubt I absolutely fell in love with it. After dedicating many years fishing I was gifted my first boat, a small aluminum jon boat, and spent almost all of my time on the water and it did not take long for me to start figuring out where the fish are and how to get them to consistently eat. After taking many friends fishing and seeing how happy and how much fun they would have it was at that point I decided to turn my absolute passion into my life long career!

Here in Central Florida's East Coast, just a short drive from Orlando, we have a world class fishery with awesome action year round! From tailing redfish on the flats to giant tarpon right off the beach. Captain Tyler Hendricks has over 10 years of fishing experience and local knowledge in the Cocoa Beach, Merritt Island, and Titusville areas to ensure you have a great and safe day on the water!
